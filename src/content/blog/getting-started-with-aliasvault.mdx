AliasVault is a powerful tool for managing your online identities and protecting your privacy. With AliasVault, you can:

- Control your online presence
- Stay safe from spam and phishing

## Getting Started

To get started with AliasVault, follow these simple steps:

1. Sign up for an account
2. Create your first email alias
3. Start using your alias for online services
4. Monitor and manage your aliases

## Best Practices

Here are some best practices for using AliasVault:

- Use unique aliases for different services
- Regularly review your alias usage
- Keep your account secure with strong passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
