I'm very proud to announce that **AliasVault has crossed 2.000 GitHub stars**. Thank you for the continued support and trust, seeing more and more people finding out about AliasVault really motivates us!

## New recent milestones

Since reaching 1.500 GitHub stars in October, we've launched a lot of new features based on your feedback:

- **Passkey support**: create and use passkeys across browser extension and mobile apps
- **Login with mobile app**: use your phone to unlock and authenticate your vault
- **Major datamodel update**: enabling custom fields, more login types (e.g. credit card, secure note), folders, field history, and recently deleted items
- **Full offline mode**: view and edit your vault offline in the extension and mobile apps, with automatic sync when you're back online
- **Even more languages**: thanks to your help, AliasVault is now available in **16 languages** (!), plus additional options in the identity generator

Your help really enables AliasVault to be a success!

## What's Coming

Read up on our [mission and roadmap](/mission) to see where AliasVault is headed.

We're currently focused on **adding more polish** to all AliasVault apps to make day-to-day use even easier, and we actively listen to your feedback. If you have any suggestions or feedback, feel free to [join our Discord](https://discord.gg/DsaXMTEtpF), [create a feature request on GitHub](https://github.com/aliasvault/aliasvault/issues), or [contact us via email](https://www.aliasvault.net/contact).

**In the coming months** we're working on:

- **Vault sharing**: share vaults with multiple people
- **More authentication options**
- **v1.0**: we expect to have v1.0 ready in a few months

Check out the [v1.0 roadmap](https://github.com/aliasvault/aliasvault/issues/731) for the full picture about what will be worked on.
